Roast Dinner

English style juicy and delicious

Use just one pan to make a complete roasted chicken dinner with roasted carrots, potatoes and a creamy garlic herb gravy"

Ingredients

  • 1 x 1.4kg whole free-range chicken
  • 1 lemon
  • 4 fresh bay leaves
  • 2 bunchs of fresh soft herbs
  • olive oil
  • 2 sprigs of fresh reosemary

Steps

  1. Preheats the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. Place chicken legs in a large bowl. Spread chili powder, garlic pwder, cayenne, and salt onto the chicken; pour sauce over top. Mix thoroughly and transfer to a baking sheet.
  3. Bake, uncovered, in the preheated oven until chicken is no longer pink at the bone and the juices run clear, 45 to 50 minutes. An instant-read thermometer inserted near the bone should read 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).
